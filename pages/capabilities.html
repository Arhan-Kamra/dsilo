AI Platform

DSilo: The future of Enterprise Contract Value Management Performance

Uniquely powerful solutions empowering business users.

dSilo’s deep-learning proprietary AI technology extracts contextualized information from all business contracts, financial documents; and other unstructured documents in compliance with enterprise policies and standards. Providing actionable reconciliation and information, DSilo’s contextualized intelligence extraction helps reveal value typically lost during complex interactions with suppliers, customers and partners, and within various transaction systems.

capabilities

dSilo’s AI platform capabilities are the foundation for enabling cutting-edge solutions for extracting and combining both structured and un-structured data into valuable insights to help harness unrealized value which may otherwise be lost.

Extraction.ai

dSilo’s intelligent digitization and extraction drives knowledge creation and retention, delivers contextual insights, and provides new perspectives and avenues advancing your business goals. Extract insights from complex documents - contracts, invoices, purchase orders - quickly, easily, and efficiently using DSilo’s advanced AI technology, deep-learning algorithms, artificial neural networks, and natural language understanding and processing developed as a result of years of rigorous training and testing by industry domain experts.

Reconciliation.ai

Identify value opportunities and reconcile documents with transactions, spend data, and financial systems. Analyze spend data and drive cost savings with dSilo’s categorization and AI-driven views. Prebuilt auto matching algorithms to match variety of supplier transactional data with contractual commitments.

Actions.ai

Generate automated and self-executing actions based on custom-defined contextual insights and reconciliation rules and processes, enabling a balanced and synergistic combination of AI and human intervention for efficient workflow and effective decision making. Automate actions, build and apply business rules, and create appropriate automated actions, and empower business users to define and configure their own rules and actions based on contextual understanding of contract metadata and transactional data.
